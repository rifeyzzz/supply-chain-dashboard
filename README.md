# Supply Chain Performance Dashboard & Strategic Analysis

**Author:** El Ouriachi Iliass | M1 BDEEM — Université Marie et Louis Pasteur  
**Tools:** Power BI | Excel | DAX | Star Schema  
**Domain:** E-Commerce | Supply Chain Analytics | Business Intelligence

---

## Overview
Analysis of 180,519 e-commerce transactions across 5 global markets 
(Europe, LATAM, Pacific Asia, USCA, Africa) covering sales performance, 
delivery efficiency, profit margins and 2019 forecasting.

---

## Dashboard Architecture
- **Page 1 — Descriptive:** KPI cards, market performance, category sales, delivery status
- **Page 2 — Diagnostic:** Late delivery analysis, profit margins, sales-profit correlation, segment performance
- **Page 3 — Predictive:** Trend analysis, QoQ growth tracking, 2019 forecasts
- **Page 4 — Prescriptive:** Strategic targets, market-specific goals, recommendations

---

## Key Findings
- Total Revenue: **$33.05M** | Total Profit: **$3.97M** | 65.75K orders
- Late delivery rate: **54.82%** — identified as critical operational weakness
- Europe leads sales (**$9.8M**)
